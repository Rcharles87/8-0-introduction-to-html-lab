The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandhich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 3 Pieces of Bread with the crusts cut off
  • Jif® Creamy Peanut Butter
  • Smucker’s® Strawberry Jam
  • 1/2 cup of sliced Strawberries
  • A butter knife
  • A sharpe knife

Directions

  1. First you will apply thin layer of Jif® Creamy Peanut Butter on the first piece of bread using butter knife.
  2. Top with a thin layer of Smucker’s® Strawberry Jam using butter knife.
  3. On the middle piece of bread, spread thin layers of Jif® Creamy Peanut Butter on both sides of it using butter knife.
  4. Place that middle piece on top of the jam, peanut butter side will be both up and down since you covered both sides.
  5. Place fresh strawberry slices on top of the Jif® Creamy Peanut Butter.
  6. Spread a light layer of Peanut Butter on one side of the remaining piece of bread using butter knife.
  7. Lay that last piece peanut butter side down atop the strawberry slices.
  8. Use sharpe knife to cut in half.
  9. Enjoy!
